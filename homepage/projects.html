Project Location Details
SunPeak Harvest Mojave Desert, California SunPeak Harvest is a large-scale solar farm nestled in the Mojave Desert, California. With a capacity of 150 MW, it harnesses the abundant sunlight to generate clean energy, powering thousands of homes in the region.
SolarScape Oasis Outback, Australia Located in the vast Outback of Australia, SolarScape Oasis is an innovative solar project covering an area of 500 acres. It utilizes advanced solar tracking technology to maximize energy capture and enhance efficiency.
EcoSun Community Costa Rica EcoSun Community is a sustainable solar initiative in the heart of Costa Rica. This community-based project combines rooftop solar panels with energy storage, promoting clean energy adoption and reducing carbon footprint.
